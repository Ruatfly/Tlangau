How premium works

1

Install Tlangau

Download from the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play (Android).

2

Open Tlangau Premium

In the app, choose the services you need (monthly or yearly) and subscribe securely.

3

Sign in

Use Sign in with Apple (iOS) or Google (Android) with the same account used for purchase.

4

Use the server dashboard

Your entitlement activates automatically after payment verification.

Can I buy premium on this website?

No. Subscribe inside the Tlangau app via the App Store or Google Play.

How do I restore a purchase?

Open Tlangau Premium in the app and tap Restore purchases (iOS) or sign in with the same store account.
