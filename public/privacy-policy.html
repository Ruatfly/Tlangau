Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 22, 2026

1. Introduction

Tlangau ("we," "our," or "us") provides community communication and notification features through the Tlangau mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains what data we access, collect, use, and share when you use the app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account and Profile Information

  • Google account information: Name, email address, and profile details returned by Google Sign-In.
  • User-entered profile data: Contact and address details you enter in donation/payment flows.

2.2 Location Data (Important)

When you use location-related features in the app, we access and collect location information:

  • Precise location: GPS coordinates obtained from your device when you use the location picker or location-sharing features.
  • Approximate location: Network-based location where precise location is unavailable.
  • Derived address: Human-readable address generated from coordinates (reverse geocoding).

We collect location data only when you use location features. We do not collect continuous location data in the background for tracking.

2.3 Notification and Device Data

  • Push token: Firebase Cloud Messaging token used to deliver notifications.
  • Device and app metadata: Basic technical information needed for app reliability, diagnostics, and notification delivery.

2.4 Files and Media You Provide

  • Images/screenshots: For example, payment proof images you select or share into the app.
  • Audio recordings: If you choose features that record or send audio.

2.5 Donation and Transaction Data

  • Donation setup details, payer/recipient details, payment references, status, timestamps, and proof submission data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use data to provide and improve core app functionality, including:

  • Sign-in, account access, and app security.
  • Creating and managing community posts, records, polls, and donation flows.
  • Sending push notifications and reliability alerts.
  • Showing, selecting, and sharing map/location information when you request location features.
  • Reviewing payment proof and processing donation confirmation/rejection.
  • Fraud prevention, abuse prevention, legal compliance, and support.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only as needed to operate the app:

  • Google Sign-In: Authentication and account identity.
  • Firebase services: Realtime Database, Cloud Messaging, and Storage for app data and notifications.
  • Image hosting/upload providers: For user-submitted proof images.
  • Map/geocoding/search providers: To return map, place, and address results for location features.
  • Legal reasons: If required by law or to protect safety and rights.

5. Location Data Disclosure Summary

Location data is accessed and/or collected when you use location features such as map selection, nearby places, or location sharing. We use this data to:

  • Show your current position on the map.
  • Let you pick and share a location.
  • Convert coordinates to addresses and improve location relevance.

Location sharing occurs through app functionality you trigger. You can stop location access by denying permission in device settings.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data in transit and at rest. No system can guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide app features and maintain records.
  • Comply with legal obligations.
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or object to processing of your personal data. You may also request account/data deletion.
