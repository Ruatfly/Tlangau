Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 13, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Tlangau ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service to purchase server access codes.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you purchase an access code:

  • Email Address: Required to send your access code and transaction confirmations
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through our payment gateway partners (we do not store full payment card details)
  • Transaction Details: Order ID, payment status, and transaction timestamps

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain information when you visit our website:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring website addresses

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To process your access code purchase and complete transactions
  • To send your access code and transaction confirmations via email
  • To verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • To improve our website and services
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To analyze website usage and trends

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Payment Processors: We share necessary payment information with our payment gateway partners to process transactions
  • Email Service Providers: We use email service providers to send access codes and confirmations
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your information may be transferred

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of data in transit using SSL/TLS
  • Secure storage of access codes and transaction records
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements

Transaction records and access code information are retained for a minimum period as required by law and for operational purposes.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)

  • Data Portability: Request transfer of your data
